Forward Kinematics: Chaining Matrices

3d_rotation Drag to rotate
Final Pose of Robot Tip (T_final)

As you move the sliders below, the system computes the Forward Kinematics.

1. It calculates a Transformation Matrix for Joint 1.

2. It calculates a Transformation Matrix for Joint 2.

3. It multiplies them: T_final = T_1 × T_2 × T_tip

The blue numbers (top-left 3x3) represent the 3D Rotation (pointing direction) of the tip.

The yellow numbers (top-right 3x1) represent the exact X, Y, Z coordinates of the tip in space!
